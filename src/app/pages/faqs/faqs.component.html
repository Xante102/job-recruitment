How do i post a job on Amber Employ?

Here are the steps to manually post a job in your Amber Employ account
1. Under Publish Jobs in your accout, click Post New Job.
2. Complete the required feilds, including Job Title, Job Description, and Job Location.
3. Click Proceed to Next Step.
4. Complete any of the remaining optional feilds to add more details to your job posting, remaining feilds.
(Note: the more information you including, the more likely candidates are to apply!)
5. Click Save and Publish
